About us

Who are we?

Created by Team Fluid, fitSENSEI is a training platform featuring both online and in-person programs designed to help you achieve peak health while having fun. Our mission is to make holistic fitness manageable and enjoyable — better health means a better life for us and those around us. We offer programs across posture and corrective exercises, strength training and cardio, nutrition, and mobility. Think of us as a university of feeling great!
